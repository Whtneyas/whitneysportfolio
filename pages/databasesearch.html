INTRODUCTION

Ecoinvent is one of the most comprehensive databases for environmental and life cycle data. It‚Äôs widely used by sustainability professionals, researchers, and policy makers. However, many users reported that finding the right dataset within Ecoinvent was time-consuming and confusing. As part of this project, I set out to redesign the user experience of dataset discovery ‚Äî helping users navigate complex data structures more efficiently and confidently.

ABOUT CLIENT

Ecochain is a company specialized in Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) ‚Äî a method used to measure the environmental impact of a product throughout its entire life cycleEcochain offers two LCA software solutions: Ecochain Mobius and Ecochain Helix. The main difference between the two lies in their target users and applications: Ecochain Mobius promotes ecodesign and is designed for product designers with limited knowledge of LCA. It enables them to easily create LCAs and make more sustainable design choices early in the development process. Ecochain Helix empowers companies to create LCAs at scale for their production facilities. It provides insights into environmental impacts at the company, process, and product level, supporting strategic sustainability decisions.

Expert review

To understand the current state of the product, I performed an exploratory product analysis using a heuristic evaluation. This allowed me to identify initial usability issues and form assumptions for further user research.

Best practices/Design patterns

Before I began designing, I conducted a best-practices analysis. I examined how other websites implement filters and where these filters are positioned on the page. I also looked at how they allow users to compare products. For this research, I primarily analysed e-commerce platforms such as bol.com, MediaMarkt, Tweakers, Coolblue, Amazon, and around ten additional e-commerce websites. I also reviewed relevant articles on optimising websites through filtering and the differences between using filters versus a search bar on a website..

Competitor research

To gain insight into how competitors use filters in their software, I conducted a competitive analysis by examining similar software products. I researched the following competitors, and the insights I gathered were applied in my low-fidelity sketches.

  • Picklr
  • One Click LCA
  • Earthster

PROPOSED SOLUTION

The solution provides a more precise search system, improves the ordering of search results through the use of filters, and enables users to compare datasets.
